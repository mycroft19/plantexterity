About Us

Who We Are

Founded by Rashmi (MSc in Botany) and Nishant (MSc in Microbiology), our lab focuses on bridging the gap between academic research and real-world applications in plant tissue culture, aquaculture and biotechnology.

🎯

Our Mission

To develop sustainable, scalable, and scientifically validated solutions for:

  • Plant propagation systems
  • Aquaculture production
  • Algal biotechnology
🔭

Our Vision

To become a leading center for business, research, training, and innovation.
